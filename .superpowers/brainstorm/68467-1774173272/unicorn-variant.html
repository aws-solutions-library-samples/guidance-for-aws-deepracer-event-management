Which Unicorn display are you using?

Each variant has a different LED matrix size — this shapes what we can show and how.

53 × 11 LEDs — landscape strip

Galactic Unicorn

Wide landscape strip — ideal for scrolling text, leaderboard ticker, and lap time display. Best for mounting along a barrier or desk edge. Limited to ~2 lines of content at once.

32 × 32 LEDs — square panel

Cosmic Unicorn

Square panel — fits 4–5 lines of content. Can show position, racer name, current lap time, and best lap simultaneously. Great all-rounder for a race display.

16 × 16 LEDs — compact square

Stellar Unicorn

Compact square — tight pixel space, only 2–3 short data items visible at once. Best for simple status indicators or a single lap time. Challenging for names.
